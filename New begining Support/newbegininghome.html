About NewBegining

NewBegining is a non-governmental social intervention dedicated to giving the survivors of the Bok Haram insurgents a fresh start in life, we at New Beginning have taken up the task of contributing to building Nigeria by providing every kind of support to the displaced persons across different IDP. If you have been to any IDP camp or seen any of them from our images, you will know that much is needed to give the campers a feeling of inclusiveness, emotional stability, and health and sanitation services. Many of the campers are traditionally farmers and traders, being uprooted from their home overnight leaves them with nothing to start life with in the camp. Our support services for the IDPs includes the following.

  • Provision of medical team to the IDP camp giving attention to both physical medical condition as well as psychological reset for the traumatic experience.
  • Teachers to man the makes shift schools at the IDP where children are been taught and adult are trained how to live as IDPs.
  • Provision of food, toiletries & other necessary consumables to augment the running of the IDP
